Glow in the Dark Balloon Dog

$5.99

All the fun of a classic balloon animal, without the fear of it popping! By day, it's a cheerful, modern sculpture in your choice of five vibrant colors. But when the lights go down, the real magic begins! After charging in the light, your balloon dog will cast a cool, luminous glow.
